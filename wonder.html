Book Review: Wonder

Book Details

Author: R.J. Palacio
Publishing: Alfred A. Knopf
Country: United States
Genre: Fiction
Pages: 320

About the Author

R.J. Palacio is an American author and graphic designer. Wonder is her first novel.

Publishing Information

Wonder was published in 2012 by Alfred A. Knopf. It has since become a New York Times bestseller and has been adapted into a film.

Summary

Wonder is a book by R.J. Palacio about a boy named August Pullman who has facial differences and struggles to fit in at a new school.

Reviews

"Wonder is a beautifully written, heartwarming story that teaches important lessons about empathy and acceptance." - The Guardian
